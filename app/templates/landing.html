Capabilities

Everything your department needs

Purpose-built modules covering the full forensic medicine workflow — from patient intake to final report.

Case Management

Track medico-legal cases from intake through closure with structured data capture and status workflows.

Postmortem Records

Document complete autopsy details including cause of death, injuries and toxicology requests.

Clinical Examinations

Record physical examination findings, injury mapping and provisional diagnoses for living patients.

Specimen & Evidence

Full chain-of-custody tracking for biological specimens and forensic evidence items.

Lab Requests & Results

Submit lab analysis requests, record results and link findings back to cases automatically.

Report Generation

Draft, review and submit medico-legal reports with approval workflow and print-ready layouts.
